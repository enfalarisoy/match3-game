Color Slide Match 3

Slide tiles one space, create matches, and clear every cracked cell before you run out of moves.

How To Play

Learn The Rules

Goal

Clear all 12 cracked obstacle cells before your 24 moves run out.

Basic Tiles

Match 3 or more tiles of the same color in a row or column to clear them.

Obstacle Tiles

Cracked striped tiles are obstacles. They disappear only when that tile is part of a clear.

How A Move Works

Swipe one tile exactly one space up, down, left, or right. Diagonal moves are not allowed.

If the swap does not create a match, it snaps back and does not spend a move.

Extra Clear Patterns

Connected group clear

Watch the center blue tile move up one space to create a 3-match. After that match is formed, the blue tiles touching it by shared edges disappear too.

The top row is the direct match. The blue tiles below remain connected through shared sides, so they disappear in the same chain reaction.

Bonus Moves

Clear 4 to 8 tiles in one move: +1 extra move

Clear 9 or more tiles in one move: +2 extra moves

Bonus tiles count across the whole chain reaction from that move, not just the first pop.

Score

Every cleared tile is worth 10 points.
